Frequently Asked Questions

How should I store Hope Bites products?

All Hope Bites products should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 2 weeks for optimal freshness. Some products may be refrigerated for extended shelf life - check individual packaging for specific storage instructions.

Are Hope Bites products allergen-free?

We offer several allergen-free options. All products are clearly labeled with allergen information. Our facility handles nuts, dairy, and soy, so cross-contamination is possible. Please check product labels carefully if your child has food allergies.

How long does delivery typically take?

Standard delivery within Kenya takes 3-5 business days.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes! We offer quantity discounts for orders of 10+ units. Please contact our sales team at sales@hopebites.com for custom pricing on large orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, you may return unopened products within 30 days for a full refund. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.
