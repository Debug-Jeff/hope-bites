About Hope Bites

Nourishing children worldwide with nutritious and delicious snacks.

Our Story

Hope Bites was born from a student project addressing a critical need: combating the 30% child malnutrition rate in Kajiado County, Kenya. Our research showed that many families rely on basic maize flour porridge lacking essential nutrients, contributing to stunting and developmental challenges in children.

What began as a university nutrition class assignment has evolved into a mission-driven concept for a locally-sourced, fortified complementary food. Hope Bites is designed as an affordable porridge that's culturally appropriate while providing the vital nutrients children need for healthy development.

Our vision is that with every purchase of Hope Bites, we'll be able to donate nutritional supplements to communities in need through our partners, creating sustainable impact while addressing immediate nutritional deficiencies in Kajiado County and beyond.

Our Products

Each Hope Bites product is carefully formulated to address specific nutritional needs while maintaining delicious flavors that children love.

Fortified Grains

Our flagship product designed to support overall growth and development with essential proteins and minerals.

Key Ingredients:

  • Carbohydrate Blend:Sorghum, millet and other cereals for muscle development
  • Calcium & Vitamin D: For strong bone development
  • Iron: Essential for blood health and cognitive development
  • B Vitamins: Supporting energy metabolism and brain function

Brain Boost Bites

Formulated to support cognitive development and brain health with omega-3s and essential nutrients.

Key Ingredients:

  • DHA & EPA: Omega-3 fatty acids essential for brain development
  • Zinc: For immune support and cognitive processing
  • Antioxidants: From blueberry and spinach concentrates

Immune Support Bites

Designed to strengthen children's immune systems with targeted vitamins and nutrients.

Key Ingredients:

  • Vitamin C: From acerola cherry extract for immune support
  • Vitamin D3: Supporting immune regulation
  • Zinc: Essential minerals for immune function
